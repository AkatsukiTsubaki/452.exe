Developing a Smart Campus Mobile Application

My university project was centered around the development of a smart campus mobile application. The objective of this project was to enhance the student experience by providing a convenient platform for accessing campus services and information.

Latest developments

To kickstart the project, we conducted a thorough survey among students to identify their pain points and needs. We gathered feedback on various aspects such as course scheduling, library resources, campus events, and other essential services. This data was crucial in shaping the features and functionalities of our application.
