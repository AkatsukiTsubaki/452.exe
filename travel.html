Popular attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, a symbol of Paris, stands tall against the skyline, inviting you to ascend its stairs for a panoramic view of the city. The River Seine flows lazily, reflecting the grandiose facades of Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Pont Neuf bridge. A cruise along the river is a must, offering a serene way to admire the city's beauty.

Tokyo

Tokyo is also a food lover's paradise. From sushi bars to ramen shops, and from izakayas to kaiseki restaurants, the city offers a diverse array of culinary experiences. Sampling the local fare is an essential part of the Tokyo travel experience.

Recommended itinerary

Itinerary cover

Here's a recommended European travel itinerary for a brief yet memorable trip: Days 1-3: Paris, France Explore the Louvre Museum & Notre-Dame Cathedral. Ascend the Eiffel Tower for a city view. Cruise the Seine River. Enjoy French cuisine & coffee at local cafés. Days 4-5: Amsterdam, Netherlands Visit Anne Frank House & Van Gogh Museum. Explore the canals by boat. Shop at the Nine Streets for unique gifts. Sample Dutch treats like waffles & pancakes. Days 6-7: Prague, Czech Republic Admire the Charles Bridge & Prague Castle. Visit the Old Town Square & Astronomical Clock. Taste traditional Czech cuisine like goulash & beer. Enjoy a river cruise at night. Days 8-9: Rome, Italy Explore the Colosseum, Vatican City, & Sistine Chapel. Stroll through the Trevi Fountain & Spanish Steps. Taste Italian pizza & gelato. Attend a classical concert or opera. Day 10: Return Home This itinerary covers the highlights of four diverse European cities, allowing you to experience their rich history, culture, and cuisine in a short span of time. Adjust as needed to fit your interests and travel style. Buon viaggio!
